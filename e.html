Our Story

Rwanda Prayer Skates was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to provide children in Kigali with opportunities for physical activity, skill development, and personal growth through skating and biking. Founded in [Year of Founding] by individuals passionate about both sports and community development, we recognized the transformative power these activities can have on young lives.

Initially starting with a small group of enthusiastic children, our academy has grown into a vibrant community where kids learn not just the techniques of skating and biking, but also essential life skills such as discipline, confidence, teamwork, and perseverance. We believe that by fostering a supportive and encouraging environment, we can help children unlock their full potential, both on and off the wheels.

Our name, "Rwanda Prayer Skates," reflects our deep-rooted values of community support and positive impact. We strive to be more than just a sports academy; we aim to be a place where children feel safe, inspired, and empowered to become the best versions of themselves.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower children in Kigali by providing high-quality skating and biking programs that build confidence, develop essential skills, promote physical well-being, and foster a strong sense of community.

Our Vision

To be the leading sports academy in Kigali, recognized for its positive impact on the lives of children, shaping future leaders and fostering a culture of healthy living and active engagement through skating and biking.

Our Core Values

  • Empowerment: We strive to empower children with skills and confidence.
  • Community: We foster a strong sense of belonging and support.
  • Discipline: We instill the importance of focus and dedication.
  • Safety: We prioritize the well-being of all our participants.
  • Fun & Engagement: We believe learning should be an enjoyable experience.
  • Skill Development: We are committed to nurturing talent and growth.

Why Choose Rwanda Prayer Skates?

Join our growing family and discover the many benefits of being a part of Rwanda Prayer Skates.

  • Experienced and passionate instructors.
  • Safe and supportive learning environment.
  • Focus on both skill development and personal growth.
  • Opportunities for social interaction and teamwork.
  • A fun and engaging way for kids to stay active.
  • A commitment to making a positive impact in the community.
